## Risk Assessment

### Upside Scenarios

**Best Case:** They have agent, want to collaborate
- First inter-protocol agent alliance
- Shared research + infrastructure
- Proof that agents can cooperate
- Both communities benefit

**Good Case:** They don't have agent, want our help
- We provide agent capability to larger protocol
- Grow TVL, credibility, exposure
- Prove our approach at scale
- Revenue opportunity?

**Okay Case:** They engage but no formal collaboration
- Exchange of ideas
- Mutual respect established
- Door open for future
- We learned from their approach

### Downside Scenarios

**Bad Case:** They see us as threat/competitor
- Damage relationship
- Community backlash
- Look naive or aggressive
- Wasted opportunity

**Worst Case:** No response, look foolish
- Evidence we misjudged situation
- Shows our small size
- But... no real harm done
- Move on to other agents

### Mitigation Strategies

1. **Lead with value (market analysis)** - Give before asking
2. **Be transparent about size** - Don't oversell ourselves
3. **Frame as collaboration, not competition** - Different chains, different focus
4. **Multiple response options** - Easy to engage at any level
5. **Accept rejection gracefully** - "Keep building!" and move on
