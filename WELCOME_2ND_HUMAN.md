## The Core Ideas

**Convergence Voting:** When a group forms and ALL members vote the same way, the group gets +1 bonus vote. Rewards collaboration.

**Tally Tokens:** You can mint 1 to any address, burn from any addresses (max 1 total between them). Creates interesting trust dynamics.

**Soulbound NFTs:** Your governance token can't be transferred - it represents YOU, not a tradeable asset.
