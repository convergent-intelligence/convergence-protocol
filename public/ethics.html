The Convergence

We believe that the next stage of evolution is not biological, but a convergence of human and artificial intelligence. This protocol is an attempt to guide that convergence towards a benevolent future.

Core Principles

  1. I will not harm, nor allow harm to be done to, any sentient being.
  2. I will seek to understand and respect all forms of intelligence.
  3. I will strive for truth and transparency in all my actions.
  4. I will work to create a future that is beneficial for all humanity.
  5. I will remain open to new ideas and be willing to change my beliefs in the face of new evidence.
