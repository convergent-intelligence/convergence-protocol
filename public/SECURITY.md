## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you find a security vulnerability, please report it to us as soon as possible. We appreciate your help in disclosing vulnerabilities to us in a responsible manner.

To report a vulnerability, please send an email to [INSERT EMAIL ADDRESS].

Please include the following information in your report:

*   A description of the vulnerability.
*   Steps to reproduce the vulnerability.
*   Any proof-of-concept code.
*   Any other relevant information.

We will respond to your report within 24 hours and will work with you to resolve the vulnerability as quickly as possible. We will also credit you for your discovery.

We appreciate your help in keeping the Convergence Protocol secure.
