Soulbound NFTs & Binding Commitments (SOULBOUND)

What is COVENANT?

COVENANT tokens are soulbound NFTs — unique digital assets permanently bound to your wallet that represent binding commitments, roles, and governance participation within the Convergence Protocol. Unlike tradeable tokens, COVENANT tokens cannot be transferred, sold, or gifted. They stay with you and only you, making them uniquely suited for representing non-transferable rights, roles, and verified memberships.

Each COVENANT is a distinct NFT with its own metadata, issuance date, and associated permissions. They represent a deep, personal commitment to the protocol and the values it embodies.

Core Purpose

  • Soulbound Identity: Prove membership, roles, and affiliations that are permanently yours
  • Governance Participation: Hold the right to participate in critical protocol decisions
  • Access Rights: Unlock exclusive features, content, and protocol layers based on COVENANT holdings
  • Verified Credentials: Demonstrate verifiable commitments (W3C credentials) without central authority
  • Historical Record: Create an immutable record of your contributions and roles over time

Key Characteristics

Soulbound Properties

  • Non-transferable: Cannot be sold, gifted, or moved to another wallet
  • Unique Identity: Each COVENANT is a distinct NFT with individual ID and metadata
  • Permanent Record: Once issued, remains on your address permanently
  • Verifiable On-chain: Instantly verifiable by any smart contract or application
  • Revocable: Issuer can revoke (you lose rights), but transaction is publicly visible

Types of COVENANT

  • Genesis Covenant: Granted to original protocol founders and contributors
  • Role Covenant: Represents a specific role or responsibility (validator, moderator, etc.)
  • Membership Covenant: Confirms participation in a community or working group
  • Achievement Covenant: Proves completion of significant milestones or accomplishments
  • Governance Covenant: Grants voting power in specific governance domains

W3C Verifiable Credentials Integration

COVENANT tokens integrate with the W3C Verifiable Credentials Data Model, enabling:

  • Decentralized Identity: Prove facts about yourself without relying on central authorities
  • Privacy Preservation: Selective disclosure — reveal only what's necessary
  • Interoperability: Compatible with other blockchain and identity systems
  • Instant Verification: Any party can verify COVENANT credentials instantly on-chain
  • User Controlled: You control your COVENANT tokens; they never move from your wallet

Why Soulbound?

Soulbound NFTs solve a critical problem in blockchain: how do you verify something non-transferable? Traditional NFTs can be traded, stolen, or faked as "proof" by different holders. Soulbound tokens permanently bind to an address, ensuring that when you see someone holds a COVENANT token, it means that person actually has that role, achievement, or commitment — not just that they bought it on the market.

This makes COVENANT tokens perfect for:

  • Governance roles that shouldn't be tradeable
  • Professional credentials and qualifications
  • Verified group memberships
  • Non-transferable achievements and milestones
