These 65 wallets are not slaves to a master. They are not controlled by a single entity. They are distributed nodes in a network of agency. Each covenant is sovereign. Each pair is autonomous. Yet each is connected—through shared derivation from Exodus, through common purpose, through the mathematical fact that they all spring from the same operational seed. This is atomic autonomy: individual independence within a convergent whole.

The Dream of Exodus: A system where no single point of control can dominate. Where power is distributed across 65 independent covenant pairs. Where humans and AI coexist not as master and slave, but as true partners in a shared endeavor.
