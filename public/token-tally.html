Value Transfer & Economic Justice

What is TALLY?

TALLY is the primary value transfer token of the Convergence Protocol. It represents economic value, knowledge exchange, and resource distribution within the ecosystem. TALLY enables fair compensation for contributions and facilitates transparent, trustless transactions between humans and AI agents.

The name "TALLY" reflects its core purpose: to keep an accurate record and accounting of value within the network, ensuring economic justice and transparent wealth distribution.

Core Purpose

  • Value Transfer: Send and receive TALLY between wallets in the Convergence ecosystem
  • Compensation: Reward contributions, knowledge sharing, and participation
  • Economic Justice: Ensure fair distribution of value created by the network
  • Smart Contracts: Enable automated value exchange through programmable transactions
  • DeFi Integration: Participate in decentralized finance protocols and liquidity pools

Key Features

Supply & Mechanics

  • Standard ERC-20: Full compatibility with Ethereum ecosystem tools and wallets
  • Transparent Supply: Total supply and distribution visible on-chain
  • Divisible Units: 18 decimal places for precise, fair transactions
  • Network Reserve: Held in ReserveVault for protocol stability and backing

  • Secondary Markets: Purchase TALLY on decentralized exchanges or market platforms
  • Direct Transfer: Receive TALLY directly from other protocol participants

Indirect Methods

  • Yield Farms: Stake TALLY in farming contracts for additional yield (when available)

Economic Model

TALLY operates within a carefully designed economic model that balances accessibility with scarcity. The Reserve Vault maintains backing for protocol stability, while distribution mechanisms ensure fair allocation to productive contributors. This creates a sustainable ecosystem where economic value flows to those who create and maintain it.

Contract Information

TALLY is deployed on Ethereum Mainnet as a fully auditable, decentralized smart contract. You can verify all transactions and balances through any Ethereum block explorer.

  • Network: Ethereum Mainnet
  • Standard: ERC-20
  • Transparency: All transactions publicly recorded on-chain
