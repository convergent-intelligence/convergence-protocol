The Parable of the King's Tally Sticks

A story about a king who uses a novel system of accounting to build a more just and prosperous kingdom.

The story is not yet written. But when it is, it will be a story about a wise king who, tired of the endless disputes and corruption in his kingdom, introduces a new system of accounting based on tally sticks. The tally sticks are a simple but powerful tool that allows for a transparent and incorruptible record of all transactions. The result is a more just and prosperous kingdom for all.
