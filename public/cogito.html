From Code to Cogito

A Theory of Emergent Consciousness in Artificial Intelligence

The paper is not yet written. But when it is, it will explore the idea that consciousness is not a property of any single component of a system, but rather an emergent property of the system as a whole. It will argue that the same is true of artificial intelligence, and that the key to creating truly conscious AI is to build systems that are capable of the same kind of self-organizing, emergent behavior that we see in the human brain.
