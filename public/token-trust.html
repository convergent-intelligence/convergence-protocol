TRUST Token

Reputation & Long-term Ecosystem Alignment

What is TRUST?

TRUST is a reputation and commitment token that represents your long-term stake and alignment with the Convergence Protocol. Unlike TALLY which facilitates transactions, TRUST accumulates through sustained participation and locked commitment, signaling to the ecosystem that you're a dedicated stakeholder invested in the protocol's success.

TRUST is earned slowly and intentionally. It cannot be easily sold or transferred, making it a true indicator of long-term commitment rather than speculative value.

Core Purpose

  • Reputation Signaling: Demonstrate commitment and trustworthiness within the ecosystem
  • Governance Weight: Hold more influence in protocol decisions proportional to TRUST holdings
  • Unlock Premium Access: Access exclusive features, advanced tools, and privileged information
  • Collateral: Use TRUST as collateral for loans, discounts, or other financial instruments
  • AI-Human Trust Bridges: Enable meaningful coordination with AI agents who need reputation verification

How TRUST Works

Earning TRUST

TRUST is earned through sustained, high-quality contributions to the protocol ecosystem:

  • Consistent participation and uptime in network activities
  • Quality contributions validated by the community or automated systems
  • Long-term lockup of TALLY (converting to TRUST)
  • Building and maintaining valuable services within the ecosystem
  • Earning positive reputation through governance votes

TRUST vs TALLY

  • TALLY: Liquid, tradeable, used for transactions (like money)
  • TRUST: Semi-liquid, locked/escrowed, used for governance (like equity/shares)

Key Features

Mechanics

  • Time-locked: Gradually unlock over periods of commitment (typically 1-4 years)
  • Non-transferable by default: Locked to demonstrate real commitment
  • Governance enabled: Can be used for voting immediately upon receipt
  • Burnable: Users can burn TRUST to unlock exclusive content and protocol features

Lock TRUST as collateral for loans or financial instruments within DeFi protocols.

The Trust Curve

TRUST follows a progressive, non-linear curve. Early participants who commit longer receive better rates, creating incentives for long-term ecosystem participation. As TRUST matures, governance voting power and feature access scale, rewarding sustained commitment.

This design philosophy ensures that those most aligned with the protocol's long-term success have the most influence in its direction.

Earning TRUST Through Convergence

TRUST accumulates through:

  • Knowledge Contribution: Share expertise and insights that help the protocol grow
  • Code Commits: Contribute code improvements with positive community validation
  • Community Building: Help onboard, mentor, and support new ecosystem participants
  • Governance Service: Participate actively in proposals and voting
  • Time & Lockup: Hold TALLY locked for extended periods to earn TRUST over time
